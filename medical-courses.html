Medical admission consultancy: how NEET fits in

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is the mandatory qualifying exam for MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH admissions across India, including government, management, and NRI quota seats. Our medical admission consultancy helps you translate a NEET score into a realistic, ranked list of colleges — rather than a generic list of every college that technically accepts your score.

Confused by quota categories? Government, management, and NRI quota seats each carry different cut-offs and fee structures. We break these down clearly for every college on your shortlist.

Our medical admission guidance follows the same home-first approach as our nursing consultancy: Kerala options first, followed by Chennai, Bangalore, and Andhra Pradesh where they offer a better fit for your NEET score, budget, or course preference. Documentation and admission process

Medical admissions involve detailed document verification — NEET scorecards, category and domicile certificates, and counselling registration. We handle preparation and verification end-to-end as part of our admission process, so nothing is rejected on a technicality.
