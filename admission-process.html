Our four-step admission process

  • 1

    Counselling session

    We start by understanding your marks, budget, family preferences, and what you actually want from a career — not just which course is trending. This session covers nursing, medical, and allied health options as relevant to you.

  • 2

    College shortlisting

    You receive a realistic shortlist ranked by your priorities: Kerala first, then Bangalore and Chennai, then the rest of India or abroad.

  • 3

    Application & documentation

    We prepare and verify every form, certificate, and entrance exam registration so nothing is rejected on a technicality.

  • 4

    Admission & settling in

    Once you're admitted, we help with hostel arrangements, travel, and — for international placements — visa and pre-departure briefing.

Documents typically required

DocumentWhy it's needed

Not sure what you'll need? Document requirements vary by course and college. We give you a personalised checklist during your counselling session.

What makes our admission process different

Many students come to us after being confused by conflicting information from multiple sources. Because we work directly with colleges across Kerala, Chennai, Bangalore, and Andhra Pradesh, our admission process is built on current, verified information — not guesswork or outdated brochures.
