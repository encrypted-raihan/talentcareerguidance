Where we place you

Home first. Then the rest of India. Then the world.

We built our network the way a student actually plans a career — starting close to home and widening outward. That order shapes everything we recommend.

First priority

Kerala

Our home ground. Direct relationships with nursing colleges, engineering institutes, and self-financing colleges across Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, Thrissur, Kozhikode, and Kollam mean we usually know about a seat before it's publicly advertised.

  • B.Sc & M.Sc Nursing
  • KEAM-linked Engineering
  • Paramedical & Allied Health

Second priority

Bangalore & Chennai

When the right course or seat isn't available at home, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu open up far more capacity — especially in engineering, nursing, and management. We coordinate hostel placement and local guardianship for students moving away from home for the first time.

  • Deemed University Engineering
  • Private Nursing Colleges
  • MBA & Allied Courses

Third priority

Other Indian States

For specialised programs, scholarships, or simply better odds, we extend our search to institutes in Maharashtra, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and beyond — vetted the same way as our home-state partners.

  • Specialised Engineering Branches
  • Central & Deemed Universities
  • Merit Scholarship Mapping

Beyond India

Study & work abroad

A nursing degree from Kerala can lead to a hospital ward in Frankfurt.

Our international desk works with students and qualified nurses who want to study or work overseas. We don't just book a test date — we plan the whole route: which country fits your goals, what the licensing process actually requires, and how to get there without being misled by intermediaries.

Test Preparation

Country & Course Selection

Documentation & Attestation

Visa Guidance

How it works

One conversation in our office. A plan you can actually follow.

Step 1

Counselling Session

We start by understanding your marks, budget, family preferences, and what you actually want from a career — not just which course is trending.

Step 2

College Shortlisting

You receive a realistic shortlist ranked by your priorities: Kerala first, then Bangalore and Chennai, then the rest of India or abroad.

Step 3

Application & Documentation

We prepare and verify every form, certificate, and entrance exam registration so nothing is rejected on a technicality.

Step 4

Admission & Settling In

Once you're admitted, we help with hostel arrangements, travel, and — for international placements — visa and pre-departure briefing.

About the academy

Twenty-three years of sitting on the same side of the table as the student.

Talent Career Guidance International Academy was founded in 2001 to do one thing well: help students and families cut through confusing admission processes and find the course and college that actually fits them. What started as nursing and engineering counselling in Kerala has grown into a full admissions and study-abroad practice — but the approach hasn't changed. We recommend what's right for the student, not what pays the highest commission.

Direct college relationships

We work with institutions directly, so the information you get is current and accurate.

Transparent guidance

Costs, odds, and timelines explained clearly — no overselling a seat that doesn't fit.

Support beyond admission

From hostel arrangements to visa interviews, we stay involved after the offer letter arrives.
