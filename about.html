How a Kerala nursing and engineering consultancy became a full admissions practice

Talent career guidance was founded in 2001 in Kottarakkara, Kerala, with a simple goal: help students and families cut through confusing college admission processes and find a course that actually fits the student, not just a seat that fills a quota. Our first cases were B.Sc Nursing and engineering admissions for local families — and nursing admission guidance remains the heart of what we do today.

Over more than two decades, that early nursing and admission consultancy work grew into a complete education consultancy covering medical courses, paramedical and allied health programs, and study-and-work-abroad pathways for nursing graduates. What hasn't changed is our approach: direct relationships with colleges, transparent guidance on costs and odds, and support that continues well after the admission letter arrives.

Why families in Kerala, Chennai, and Bangalore choose us

Parents considering a nursing admission consultancy for their child are usually weighing a mix of concerns — genuine college seats, honest fee information, realistic odds based on marks, and support once their son or daughter is living away from home for the first time. Our admission consultancy in Kerala was built around exactly these concerns.

  • Direct college relationships across Kerala, Chennai, Bangalore, and Andhra Pradesh, so the information you receive about nursing, medical, and allied health seats is current and accurate.
  • Transparent counselling — we explain costs, realistic odds, and timelines clearly, and we won't oversell a seat that doesn't fit your marks or budget.
  • Support beyond admission — from hostel arrangements and local guardianship to, for international placements, visa interviews and pre-departure briefing.

Our focus: B.Sc Nursing, medical, and allied health admissions

While we guide students across several academic streams, our strongest focus and deepest college network is in B.Sc Nursing, medical (MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH streams), and paramedical and allied health courses — in Kerala and beyond. This concentration means our counsellors know entrance requirements, fee structures, and seat availability for nursing and medical admission in far more detail than a generalist consultancy could.

Our service area

Our admissions network covers Kerala first — including Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Kochi, Thrissur, and Kozhikode — followed by Chennai, Bangalore, and Andhra Pradesh, and extending to study and work-abroad placements in the UK, Germany, Australia, Canada, and the Gulf.
