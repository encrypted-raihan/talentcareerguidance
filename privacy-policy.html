Introduction

Talent career guidance ("we", "us", "our") operates this website to provide information and admission counselling services related to B.Sc Nursing, medical, paramedical, and allied health courses, along with study-abroad guidance. This policy explains what information we collect through the website and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, course and location preference, and any message submitted through our enquiry form or WhatsApp.
  • Basic technical information collected automatically by standard web hosting and analytics tools, such as browser type and pages visited, used only to understand and improve site performance.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide admission counselling relevant to your goals.
  • To share relevant updates about courses, colleges, or admission timelines you've asked about.
  • To improve our website content and services.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Information you share may be discussed with partner colleges or institutions strictly for the purpose of processing your admission enquiry, and only with your knowledge.

Enquiry form & WhatsApp submissions

When you submit our contact form, your details are formatted into a message and sent via WhatsApp to our counselling team. We use this information solely to follow up on your enquiry.

Cookies & analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand overall visitor behaviour and improve site performance. These do not identify you personally.

Data retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide counselling support and follow-up, or until you ask us to delete it.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
